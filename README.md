## What is Luna?

Luna is a **free, offline-first period and menstrual cycle tracker** built for women and girls in India. It works completely without the internet — your health data never leaves your phone unless you choose to back it up.

Luna learns your cycle over time using a Bayesian prediction engine, surfaces symptom and mood patterns, and gives phase-specific wellness tips — all without sending your data to any server.

**Step 2 — Allow installation from unknown sources**

Android blocks APKs that didn't come from the Play Store by default. You only need to do this once:

- **Android 8 and above** — When you open the APK, Android will ask *"Allow from this source?"*. Tap **Allow**. You can revoke this permission after installing.
- **Android 7 and below** — Go to **Settings → Security → Unknown sources** → turn on.

<br>

**Step 3 — Open and install**

Open the downloaded `app-release.apk` file from your notifications or Downloads folder. Tap **Install**.

<br>

**Step 4 — Automatic updates**

Once installed, Luna checks for updates every time you open the app. When a new version is available, you'll see an update prompt inside the app — no manual downloading needed.

## Privacy

Luna is built with privacy as the default, not an afterthought.

- **Your data stays on your device.** Cycle logs, symptoms, and mood entries are stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database.
- **No account required.** Luna works fully offline without signing in.
- **Cloud sync is opt-in.** If you choose to back up your data, it's tied to your email address via Firebase Authentication. You can delete your account and all cloud data at any time from Settings.
- **No tracking, no ads, no analytics.** Luna does not collect usage data or serve ads.
- **No hidden API calls.** The AI chatbot feature (coming soon) is hidden by default. When enabled, it routes through a server-side proxy — your conversation is never logged.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Luna free?**
Yes. Luna is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

**Does it work without internet?**
Yes. All core features — tracking, predictions, insights, tips — work fully offline.

**Is my data safe if I lose my phone?**
If you sign in with an email address (Settings → Account), your data is backed up to Firebase. Without sign-in, data is local only.

**Can I export my data?**
Yes. Settings → Data → Export all data (JSON), Export cycle history (CSV), or Doctor report (PDF).

**Does Luna support irregular cycles?**
Yes. The prediction engine has separate modes for PCOS, irregular cycles, and hormonal contraception.
